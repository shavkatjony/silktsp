1
Empathize
Goal: Get inside our players' heads—what excites them, what frustrates them, and how they learn best.
🗣️ Player Chats & Surveys

We asked about 15 classmates about their favorite puzzle games. They loved instant feedback (think clicking a city and seeing a line draw instantly) and a sense of story—"I want to feel like I'm on an adventure!"

🕵️ Competitive Detective Work

We played a handful of TSP demos. Common complaints: "It's just math!" or "I don't know if I did well." We knew we had to make costs and scores crystal clear and wrap it in a fun narrative.

2
Define
Goal: Pin down exactly what challenge we're solving—and how we'll know we nailed it.

🎯 Problem Statement

"Abstract math games feel cold and confusing. How can we turn TSP into a lively, memorable journey?"

3
Ideate
Goal: Brainstorm wild ideas, then pick the one that feels both thrilling and doable.

🎨 Theme Throwdown

🚚 Modern courier
🤖 Robot delivery
🚀 Space explorer
🐫 Medieval Silk Road caravan - WINNER!

⚙️ Core Mechanics Sketch

Basic Interaction

Click-to-visit cities (nodes)
Dual costs: time & risk

Dynamic Elements

Random event cards (bandit ambush, sandstorm!)
Power-ups: "Reveal Shortcut" & "Caravan Boost"

🏺 Why Silk Road?

Rich history + natural "trade" story = players feel like real merchants, not just number-crunchers.

4
Prototype
Goal: Whip up a bare-bones, playable version—fast—to see if the magic is there.

🔧 Development Timeline

V1 (5 Cities)

Static map, clickable cities, cost tally.

V2 (5 Cities + Flair)

Animated green path lines, hover tooltips for "time" and "risk," a big Validate button.

V3 (Full Features)

12 cities, event cards pop mid-journey, power-ups light up when available, mobile-friendly layout.

5
Test
Goal: Watch real people play, gather honest feedback, then polish until it sparkles.
🔧 Team Playtest

We chased every bug—no more "Can't click this city!"

👥 Classmate Trials (n=15)

What they loved:

"That sandstorm card surprised me!"
"Seeing my score jump when profit kicks in is so satisfying."

Tough spots:

"At first I wasn't sure what 'risk' meant." → Added a simple tooltip.

🔧 Key Tweaks

Welcome Tutorial

A quick 30-second popup walks you through your first run.

Better Contrast

City labels and tooltips now pop off the map.

Event Balance

No more constant ambushes—just enough to keep players on their toes.
