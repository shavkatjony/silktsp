üéØ Game Overview

Silk Route Strategist is a strategic trading game based on the classic Traveling Salesman Problem (TSP). As a medieval caravan master, you must plan efficient routes along the ancient Silk Road to maximize profits while minimizing time and risk.

üéØ Objective: Visit all cities exactly once, return to your starting point, and achieve the highest score possible by optimizing your route for time, risk, and profit.

üéÆ How to Play

Getting Started

  • Click "Start Game" to begin your trading adventure
  • Read the tutorial modal for detailed instructions
  • Select your starting city (highlighted in blue)
  • Plan your route by clicking cities in order

Basic Controls

  • Click cities to add them to your route
  • Hover over paths to see time and risk costs
  • Use power-ups strategically for advantages
  • Click "Validate Route" when ready to finish

Game Flow

1. Route Planning Phase: Click cities to build your caravan route

2. Halfway Event: Encounter a random event card that affects your journey

3. Route Completion: Visit all cities and return to start

4. Scoring: Receive detailed performance breakdown
